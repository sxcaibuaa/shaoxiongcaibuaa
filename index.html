Biography [Resume/CV]

Huiyang Deng is currently a third year Master student in School of Automation of Science & Electrical Engineering at Beihang University . He is supervised by Associate Professer Jingmeng Liu and co-supervised by Professer Weihai Chen. Before that, he received the Bachelar degree from Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT) in 2014.

He started his research in the spherical motor in August 2014. His main research interests include but are not limited to motion control, systems under uncertainty and multi-agent coordination. He also has strong coding ability and some basic knowledge about mechine learning.
